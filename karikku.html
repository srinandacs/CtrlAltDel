KARIKKU

Facing countless hardships, Raman turned to selling tender coconut water as a way to support his family. With little to his name, he found purpose in serving his community, offering a refreshing drink to those in need. Each coconut he cracked open is more than just a business — it’s a lifeline that helps him push through tough times. His simple service has not only brought him hope but also connected him to his neighbors, turning a daily struggle into a shared moment of strength and resilience.
