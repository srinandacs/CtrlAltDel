HOMELY MEALS

Usha's journey is one of remarkable strength and resilience. After bravely battling cancer, she decided to open a roadside food shop as a means to support her recovery and rebuild her life. What started as a small effort to survive soon blossomed into a local favorite, capturing the hearts of the community. Usha’s passion for cooking and her unwavering commitment to serving others have not only helped her heal but also inspired countless individuals around her. Her story is a testament to the power of perseverance and the deep connection food has in bringing people together.
